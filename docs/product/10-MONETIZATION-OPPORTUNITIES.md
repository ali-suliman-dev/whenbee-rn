## Data we already collect (the asset)

Fully on-device: every task's **estimated vs actual minutes**, category, start/end timestamps (→ hour-of-day + weekday), **overrun reasons**, time-of-day accuracy skew, completed/abandoned status, "minutes saved," per-category bias multiplier, sharpness/tier, plan completions. In short: a **personal time-truth dataset** most apps never have because they don't capture *actual vs guessed*.

# VERSION 1 — New money from data we ALREADY collect

Ranked by willingness-to-pay strength.

## 1.1 🔥 Honest Billable Export — "Time Receipts" (STRONG WTP)

**What:** Turn the actual-minutes-per-task/category we already log into a **billable timesheet / invoice-ready export** (CSV, PDF, share) for people who bill by the hour — freelancers, consultants, lawyers, agencies, contractors.

**Why it's new:** Distinct from the shipped clinician/coach/self PDF (a *health/calibration* report). This is a *billing* artifact. We already passively capture real durations — the exact thing time-trackers charge for.

**WTP evidence (live):**
- Manual timers (Toggl, Harvest, Clockify) give the timer free but **gate billable/invoice export to paid** ($4–11/user/mo). *(high)*
- **Passive/auto trackers charge 2–3× and mostly have no free tier** — Timely $9–28, Memtime $15–30, Rize $9.99–28.99/mo. **Auto-capture → billable report IS the premium.** *(high)*
- Traction: Harvest 70k companies / $50B+ processed; Toggl 70k companies; Clockify 7M users. Freelancers "lose 10–20% of billable hours"; only ~67% of billable time gets captured. *(med)*

**Why Whenbee wins:** the timer *is* the log, and it records **honest actuals** — passive capture without a separate tracker. Prosumer segment justifies the **upper price band**.

**Retention:** recurring weekly use (invoicing cadence) — sticky, forward-acting. Good.

**Caveat:** keep it **user-owned billable**, never employer-surveillance "proof of work" (surveillance framing clashes with the no-guilt brand). Verdict: **build.**

## 1.2 👍 ADHD / Time-Blindness packaging of existing data (STRONG WTP — positioning + a focused report)

**What:** Repackage the data we ALREADY have (per-category bias, hour/weekday accuracy skew, "you underestimate X by N%") into an **ADHD/time-blindness profile** — and lead brand positioning with it. More a monetizable *framing + targeted report* than a new engine.

**WTP evidence (live):**
- **Tiimo: 50,000+ paying subs, 75%+ neurodivergent, Apple iPhone App of the Year (Dec 2025).** **Llama Life: solo founder, $51k→$135k ARR bootstrapped.** Inflow ~$14M raised. *(high / med-high)*
- ADHD app market ~$1.8–1.9B (2025) → $4.3–6.7B by 2030–33. Price band $5–16/mo, $39–54/yr. *(med)*
- **Vocabulary is a verbatim match:** "time blindness," "time optimist," "you underestimate by X%" are the audience's own words; the planning fallacy is clinically ADHD-associated. *(high)*

**Risk = crowding, not concept:** TimeBoxer ("plan vs actual… where your estimates go wrong," calls users "time optimists") and Latered (standalone Start-By) already exist. Differentiate on **execution** (frictionless on-device calibration, no-guilt companion), not the idea.

**Retention:** ADHD audience is loyal but subscription-fatigued — bound the value clearly. Verdict: **lead positioning here; ship a "time-blindness report" using existing data.** (Keep no-guilt — never "you failed.")

## 1.3 ⚠️ Calendar / Honest-Day padding — independently re-surfaced as the STRONGEST data lever

**Flag, not a recommendation.** Independent live research re-identified **calendar padding** as the single strongest way to monetize our honest-duration data — the same conclusion that originally made it THE Pro feature. **Reclaim.ai: 500k users, ~$2.1M rev, ~5% conversion, acquired by Dropbox (2024).** Sunsama/Akiflow sustain $16–34/mo *because* calendar IS the product. *(high traction; med financials)*

This was **dropped 2026-06-19** (a settled founder call). Surfacing only because the data is unambiguous and the decision predates this research — worth a conscious "still dropped?" rather than silent omission.

## 1.4 Weak / do-not (for completeness)
- **Standalone one-off "time audit" report:** no proven consumer market; the only one-time pricing in the wild is a *lifetime license to a continuous tracker* (Timing $290). Skip. *(med)*
- **Gating raw CSV/export:** table-stakes everywhere (Notion/Todoist export free); paywalling it signals lock-in and clashes with the honest brand. Keep export free; charge for the *billable formatting* (1.1). *(high)*
- **Continuous "where your time goes" analytics:** Rize proves ~$1M ARR but churns hard, and **long-range history already shipped** — overlaps. Don't rebuild.

# VERSION 2 — New features to ADD (proven WTP)

Ranked by WTP strength; brand-fit flagged.

## 2.1 🔥 On-device / privacy-framed AI insight & reflection (STRONG WTP — highest-converting category, on-brand)

**What:** AI that *reinforces the wedge*: a non-judgmental weekly **reflection** ("you underestimate admin by 40%, sharpest before noon"), AI-assisted estimate suggestions, and natural-language/voice task entry → auto-structured task ([[ties to the voice plan, docs 08/09]]).

**WTP evidence (live):**
- **AI permanently raised WTP: AI apps monetize at ~2× pre-AI ARPU**; ChatGPT normalized $20/mo. AI apps use hard paywalls (86% skip trials) and convert. *(high)*
- Auto-scheduling proof: **Motion $50M ARR, $550M valuation (2025)**; Reclaim acquired by Dropbox. *(high)*

**Brand fit:** must stay **on-device** (Apple Foundation Models — free, offline; see voice plan) or strictly **outside the guess→timer→learn loop**, framed as honest insight, **never nagging** (no-guilt invariant). The honest-number engine is already a "smart" differentiator — AI extends it, doesn't dilute it. Verdict: **build (on-device only).**

## 2.2 👍 End-to-end-encrypted backup + multi-device sync (MODERATE–STRONG WTP, opt-in only)

**What:** Optional **E2E-encrypted** cloud backup + multi-device. Today the app is on-device only → real data-loss anxiety and no iPad/new-phone story.

**WTP evidence (live):**
- Sync/backup is *the* paid hook in indie apps: **Bear** $29.99/yr (sync gated), **Obsidian Sync** $4–5/mo (E2E add-on), **Day One** $49.99/yr (sync in paid tier). Price band ~$30–50/yr = productivity median. *(high)*

**Brand fit:** sync **conflicts** with "on-device-only" unless framed exactly as Obsidian does — **"your data, end-to-end encrypted, we can't read it,"** opt-in, never analytics-in-the-cloud. Reduces churn (data-loss anxiety) and adds a clean paid lever. Verdict: **build only as opt-in E2E.**

## 2.3 🤔 Light focus session + optional soundscape (STRONG category WTP, but off-wedge)

**What:** A "focus session" wrapper on the timer with optional ambient soundscape.

**WTP evidence (live):** Functional music monetizes superbly — **Endel $15.8M rev, >1M MAU**; Brain.fm $6/mo–$90 lifetime. Forest big on gamified focus. *(high/med)*

**Brand fit:** no-guilt-compatible but **off the calibration wedge**, with ongoing audio-content/licensing cost. Verdict: **secondary** — a light companion at most, not a core bet.

## 2.4 Free retention layer (NOT paid levers)
- **Apple Watch companion** (one-tap timer on wrist): great brand fit, strong **retention/habit** driver, but **not a paywall lever** — WTP attaches to core value, not the watch surface. Build free. *(med)*
- **Widgets / Live Activity / Lock Screen** (already scaffolded): table-stakes as paid; best as a **free retention + acquisition surface that advertises Pro**, not gated. *(med)*

## 2.5 Avoid / deprioritize
- **Body doubling (Focusmate-style):** real ADHD WTP ($8/mo) but needs live matching/video — **off the on-device, privacy-first model** + large build. Park in the future partner layer.
- **Streak/accountability-pressure coaching:** **violates the no-guilt invariant.** Only the *reflection* framing survives → that's 2.1, not a coach.

## Synthesis & recommendation

**Strongest, most coherent stack (all align with on-device + no-guilt + Model B):**

| Priority | Direction | Bucket | WTP | Brand fit |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | On-device AI insight/reflection (+ voice entry) | New | Strong | High (on-device) |
| 2 | Honest Billable Export ("Time Receipts") | Existing data | Strong | High (prosumer) |
| 3 | E2E-encrypted backup + sync (opt-in) | New | Mod–Strong | OK if E2E-framed |
| 4 | ADHD/time-blindness positioning + report | Existing data | Strong | High (lead brand) |

- **Free (trust + retention), never gate:** raw export, the calibration loop, Apple Watch, widgets/Live Activity.
- **Pricing:** productivity median ~$38.42/yr; AI + billable/prosumer angles justify the upper band ($48–60/yr). Hard-ish paywall + 17–30 day trial converts best; weekly plan for the impulse segment.
- **Retention principle (skill):** forward-acting, recurring value sticks (billable cadence, weekly reflection); **one-off reports churn** — avoid them. Productivity D7 benchmark 8–10%; the team's gate is D7 ≥ 25%.
- **Conscious flag:** independent research re-nominated **calendar padding** as the #1 data-monetization lever; it remains dropped — revisit deliberately, don't ignore.
