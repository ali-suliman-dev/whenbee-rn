Light theme — DRASTIC directions (v2)

Component-level restyles, not background tweaks. Each direction changes what cards, headers, buttons, and accents ARE. All four still honor: no guilt colors, honey/amber = reward semantics, pricing/copy untouched. Where a direction bends the "one filled indigo per screen" rule, it's flagged. Mix and match is expected — say which pieces you want.

The header becomes a deep-indigo canvas (Headspace color-blocking). Honey ring glows gold on it, calendar floats over the seam. The app gets a face instead of a blank forehead.
