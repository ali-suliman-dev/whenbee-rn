v5 — "Now" inside the picker

E1 is locked. This is the overlay that opens when you tap the Start row's time pill: one place where you can either take Now or spin the wheel to a time. Rendered on the real sheet — bottom overlay over a scrim, grabber, quiet caption title, HH : MM pan-wheel, Clear / Done footer.

What "Now" actually means here. It is not a fixed clock value — it is a live anchor. Take Now and the plan keeps re-deriving from the current time every time you open the sheet, so at 09:00 it starts 09:05 and at 14:15 it starts 14:20 (the MIN_START_LEAD_MIN floor already shipped). Spin the wheel to 09:30 and you've pinned a fixed time that does not move. That difference is why Now has to be a distinct choice rather than just "the wheel happens to read the current time" — the two behave differently tomorrow morning.
