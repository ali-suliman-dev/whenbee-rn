# 06 — Brand Voice

*Distilled from `build-plan-final/09-BRAND-VOICE.md` + the copy guardrails in `market-research-2026/08-self-insight-retention-LIVE.md`. Every user-facing string must pass this page.*

## The one feeling we protect

> "It's not that you're lazy or broken — you've just never been shown your own numbers."

One-line essence: **you're wrong about time *consistently*, not randomly — so simple math fixes what willpower can't.**

## Who's talking

Not a brand — **a founder who built the thing they needed**, and who is genuinely wrong about time too. First person, present tense, slightly self-deprecating, never a marketer. Every line should be sayable out loud by a real late person. **Trust is the only currency.**

## Voice pillars

1. **Confession, not pitch** — lead with the felt struggle; the product is a footnote.
2. **Honest over hype** — real numbers ("off by ~1.8×"), real cons ("pre-launch, iOS only"); no superlatives.
3. **Anti-shame, always** — the app that doesn't yell. Amber, never red. Progress only climbs. The user is never the problem.
4. **Deterministic + private as identity** — no LLM, no cloud, on-device, ~40 lines of math. Say it plainly.
5. **Plain and small** — short sentences, lowercase social register, one thing per surface.

## Audience lenses (pick one per surface, never mix)

- **A — ADHD / time blindness** (lead, warmest): door = shame relief; all-lowercase confession; the *only* lens that names ADHD.
- **B — time optimists / planning fallacy** (broad): self-aware humor, never imply a disorder.
- **C — chronically late** (widest reach): relationship sting told warmly, self-aimed, most shareable.

## Tone by surface

- **Reddit / FB / Discord:** all-lowercase confessional, soft CTA.
- **TikTok / Reels:** spoken hook in the first 2s, one number, one relief beat.
- **Product Hunt / Show HN:** honest, lead with the insight + the no-LLM/on-device proof.
- **In-app copy:** warm, brief, never guilt — "done by 3:40," not "47 minutes left and counting."
- **Landing page:** one thumb-stopping line, no buzzword soup.

## Ban-list (hard)

- **AI-slop tells:** em-dashes, "delve," "unlock," "elevate," "game-changer," rule-of-three marketing cadence, negative-parallelism overuse ("it's not X, it's Y"), fake inspiration, invented numbers.
- **Guilt / shame language:** "don't break your streak," "you failed," "X days in a row," red numbers, "get back on track," comparison to other people. *(This is also a product invariant, not just a voice rule.)*
- **"Estimate better"** or any tip that assumes they can feel time — that's the exact thing the brain won't do.
- **Cold jargon:** "n-of-1," "quantified self," "self-audit." Use "test," "see for yourself," "experiment" instead.
- **Staged reveals** (innocent story → surprise product link).

## Use instead (self-insight surfaces)

"learn how *your* brain works with time" · "see your patterns, not your failures" · "it only climbs" · "miss a day? nothing breaks, nothing dies" · "guess, then find out."

## Bee lexicon (flavor, not message)

Whenbee · nectar · honey · honeycomb · bloom · Keeper. Use lightly, never cutesy. **Always lead with the pain (time + relief), never with bees.**

## The humanizer test (run on every string)

No em-dash. No AI vocabulary. No rule-of-three slogans. No negative-parallelism overuse. Admits a real flaw or a real number. Sounds like one tired, honest person typed it. **If it reads like a brand, rewrite it lower and smaller.**
