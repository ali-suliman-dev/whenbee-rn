Card ②

One Thing to Try

Single forward action derived from biggest drifter. Must feel like an offer, not a command. Three surface directions.

X Eyebrow · flat card ★ Rec

ONE THING TO TRY

Give Admin & email about 1.8× next week — your day stops slipping.

Consistent with all other app card language. The amber eyebrow makes it feel distinct without a separate surface treatment. Clean. Nothing fights the action text.

Y Left-border accent

ONE THING TO TRY

Give Admin & email about 1.8× next week — your day stops slipping.

Left border visually separates "this is actionable" from informational cards. Strong directional pull. Risk: the border + amber eyebrow is two amber signals on one card — check scarcity rule.

Z Amber-wash surface

ONE THING TO TRY

Give Admin & email about 1.8× next week — your day stops slipping.

The wash makes ② unmissable — it reads as "the key card" even without scrolling slowly. Risk: if card ① already has amber edge AND card ② has amber wash, the amber scarcity rule (one amber element per surface) is at risk. Only works if ① is neutral.

W Affirmation · clean week variant

ONE THING TO TRY

Hold steady. Your reads are landing — three areas in a row now within range.

When no category drifted meaningfully this week. Never omits the card — affirmation > silence for a sharp user. Tone: calm pride, not hollow praise.
