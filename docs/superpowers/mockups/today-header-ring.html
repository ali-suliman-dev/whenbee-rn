The two-icon concern — research-based options

iOS convention: the trailing (far-right) header slot is a stable nav control — Apple Fitness, Music, App Store, Photos each keep ONE circular control there, never two stacked. Two tappable circles side-by-side also creates target ambiguity (which goes where?). Two ways to honor "settings lives far-right":

R1 · Settings far-right, ring leads left
Gear sits ALONE on the far right — exactly where the thumb expects it. The honey ring becomes a status medallion leading the left, next to the greeting. Mirrors Apple Fitness (status one side, profile/settings the other). Zero ambiguity.
R2 · Merged — one far-right control
No gear in the header. The honey ring IS the single far-right control (like an avatar). Tap → honeycomb hub, where Settings also lives. Cleanest, but settings moves one level deeper — a real tradeoff.
